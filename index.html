The Night We Met

The city was a blur of neon lights and hurried footsteps, but in that small, crowded room, everything went quiet. Time seemed to slow down, stretching the moment into an eternity.

I remember the song that was playing, a faint melody weaving through the chatter. It felt like a soundtrack written just for us.

"And in that moment, I swear we were infinite."

You were standing by the window, bathed in the soft glow of a streetlamp. You laughed, a sound that cut through the noise and went straight to my heart. I knew, right then and there, that this was a beginning.

We talked for hours, about everything and nothing. The world outside faded away, leaving just the two of us in our own little universe. It wasn't just a meeting; it was a collision of worlds, a beautiful, chaotic alignment of stars.

That night wasn't just the night we met. It was the night my world changed forever.
